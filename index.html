code park

Code Park opens fun coding experiences to students in the Houston area.

We empower students to solve problems in their community and seek jobs in the growing science and technology sectors with creative, open-source classes and workshops. We are committed to making the highest quality instruction available in communities that are underserved and underrepresented in science and technology.

Wanjun

Wanjun is a lifelong learner and makerspace educator. She believes in providing meaningful hands-on learning experiences. She's been developing and teaching LEGO robotics, 3D-Printing, 3D CAD and Raspberry Pi workshops to K-12 students for six years and most recently tricked kids into learning engineering and CAD by showing them how to 3D Print their own fidget spinners.
