THE HUMIDITY'S NOT TOO BAD

We are Code Park, a group of Houston developers joining forces to create welcoming environments for anyone to learn to code for the web. Our mission is to increase developer diversity in our community so we can grow stronger.


Visibility

Increase visiblity for your particular user group. Want everyone to come out to your Erlang meetup? We'll get the word out for you.

Merge

Let our forces combine! We are here for beginners all the way to experienced. By joining up we can help reduce simple things like meeting conflicts, but also increase the number of new initiatives from a bigger pool of volunteers.

Pair

Find other developers to work with on fun projects. What better way for beginners to keep on going than to have friends working with them? Experienced developers can also benefit from working with other developers outside their skillset or by volunteering to run beginner workshops.

Publish Presentations

We want to become a body of information for meetups, which includes having a repo of awesome presentations. Our Rails Girls presentation is the first one up.
