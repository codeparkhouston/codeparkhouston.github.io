Code Park aims to prepare high school students in under-invested communities for careers in web technology. In our after-school classes, students focus on fundamental coding skills and work on real world projects. They get the hands-on experience they need to hit the ground running in this exciting and rewarding line of work.

- US News

More About Our Classes

Our students will learn and build small projects with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS in Fall 2015. Armed with the fundamentals, students will tackle Capstone projects in Spring 2016. The projects will be built pro bono for local non-profits and businesses. Particularly motivated and dedicated students will have the opportunity to continue learning in summer internships.

How You Can Help

Opportunity

If you are a company with projects you'd like done but have no time to build, we would love to work with you to scope a project to pitch to students in Spring 2016. Here are some examples of possible projects:

  • A simple front page for your business
  • An internal app for the office dishwasher status

Connect with us to brainstorm and spec your needs here.

If you are interested in having web development interns Summer of 2016, please fill the internship form.

Time

Volunteer to teach or help in general! We have flexible opportunities. Let us know your interest and availability here.

Money

Code Park Houston is a 501(c)3 dedicated to providing coding classes that are free to students in several ways. Open opportunities to our students by donating.
